My Performance

Satoshi Gogo's Eternity covered by Kaiming Tan

Performance notes

  • Tuning: standard
  • Main techniques used: Hammer-on, pull-off and harmonics
  • What I want viewers to notice: Satoshi Gogo is my favourite Japenese fingerstyle guitar player, but the guitar I was using in the video was Kotaro Oshio's.
